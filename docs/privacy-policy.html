Log Everything — Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 28, 2026

What Data We Collect

  • Log entries — text, categories, and timestamps you create.
  • Images — photos you attach to entries.
  • Voice recordings — recorded temporarily as M4A files, converted to text, then the audio file is discarded. We do not store raw audio.
  • Account information — email address, display name, and profile photo provided by Google or Apple sign-in.
  • Device identifier — a randomly generated UUID used to associate your data with your device.

How Data Is Stored

  • Locally on your device using SharedPreferences.
  • In the cloud via Firebase Firestore (entries and categories) and Firebase Storage (images), synced to your authenticated account.

Third-Party Services

  • OpenAI — entry text and attached images are sent to OpenAI for AI-powered categorization and chat. See OpenAI’s privacy policy.
  • Firebase (Google) — used for authentication, database, and file storage. See Firebase’s privacy page.
  • Google Sign-In / Apple Sign-In — used for account authentication only.

Audio & Microphone

The app requests microphone access for voice-to-text input. Audio is recorded temporarily, converted to text via a speech recognition service, and the audio file is immediately deleted. No raw audio data is stored or transmitted.

Data Deletion

You can delete all your data from within the app at any time. Deleting entries removes them from both local storage and Firebase. You may also uninstall the app to remove all local data.

Analytics & Advertising

This app does not include any analytics SDKs or advertising frameworks.

Children’s Privacy

This app is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect data from them.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy is updated, the revised version will be posted at this URL with an updated date.
