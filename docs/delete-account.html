What Data We Store

When you use Log Everything, the following data is associated with your account:

  • Firebase account — your authentication profile (email, display name, profile photo).
  • Firestore entries — your log entries, categories, and timestamps stored in Cloud Firestore.
  • Firebase Storage images — photos you attached to entries.
  • OpenAI vector store — a copy of your entries used to power AI chat queries.

How to Request Deletion

To request complete deletion of your account and all associated data, open a GitHub issue using the link below. Please include the email address associated with your account so we can locate your data.

Request Account Deletion

What Happens Next

  • We will delete your Firebase account, Firestore data, Storage files, and OpenAI vector store data.
  • Deletion will be completed within 14 days of your request.
  • You will receive a confirmation on your GitHub issue once deletion is complete.
  • Local data on your device can be removed by uninstalling the app.
